Hitman Reborn, also call the Katekyō Hitman Reborn! is a anime that made of its magna Katekyō Hitman Reborn! It is about a normal high school boy Tsunayoshi Sawada that is bad at everything, found out that he is elected to be the tenth boss of the Vongolia family from a baby tutor Reborn that find him, but actually Reborn is the greatest hitman in the family, and his job is to teach Tsunayoshi how to be a boss of the Vongolia, which is a powerful Mafia family. During this, Tsunayoshi had met different new friends that help him and being the members of Tsunayoshi's Vongolia family. The anime has a total of 203 eposide.
